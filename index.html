About Us

Welcome to Covelyne Training Institute —
a premier institution in Kenya that is TVET Accredited offering hands-on training in beauty therapy,
hairdressing, nail technology, and spa treatments.

Our mission is to empower students with modern skills and professional expertise
needed to succeed in the dynamic beauty industry.

Whether you're starting your journey or enhancing your career,
Covelyne provides quality education, experienced tutors,
and a vibrant learning environment.
